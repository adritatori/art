An Evaluation Framework for Network IDS/IPS Datasets: Leveraging MITRE ATT&CK and Industry Relevance Metrics

Adrita Rahman Tory, Khandakar Fida Hasan

Computers & Security (Elsevier) - Q1 Journal

Role: First Author | Status: Preprint on SSRN; Under review

Impact: This paper provides a systematic methodology for assessing dataset quality in cybersecurity research. The framework leverages MITRE ATT&CK techniques and industry relevance metrics to evaluate the effectiveness and applicability of Network IDS/IPS datasets, addressing a critical gap in standardized evaluation approaches.
2025 Accepted

Mind the Gap: Missing Cyber Threat Coverage in NIDS Datasets for the Energy Sector

Adrita Rahman Tory, Dr. Khondokar Fida Hasan, Md Saifur Rahman, Nickolaos Koroniotis, Mohammad Ali Moni

International Conference on Big Data, IoT and Machine Learning (ICBDIML 2025) - Elsevier

Role: First Author | Status: Accepted

Impact: This research highlighted critical dataset limitations in critical infrastructure protection, specifically focusing on the energy sector. The study identifies significant gaps in current NIDS datasets that fail to adequately represent real-world cyber threats targeting energy infrastructure, providing crucial insights for improving security measures.

Education Sciences, MDPI
